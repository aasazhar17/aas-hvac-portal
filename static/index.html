Quality Controls

Quality Assurance Process

Our commitment to precision engineering starts from raw material testing up to structural commissioning.

Compliance Audits & Standards

AAS Air Conditioning is fully registered under ISO 9001:2015. We adhere to a rigid quality policy that checks the design layout, metal finishes, welding strength, and thermal rating of our finished grills, ducts, and cooling towers.

Every chiller unit undergoes rigorous leakage testing using electronic halogen detectors to avoid refrigerant loss. Copper joints are verified under dry nitrogen pressure up to 450 PSI prior to assembly.

1. Raw Material Verification

We source only high-density PUF chemicals, branded motors, and prime quality G.I. sheet metal with standard zinc coating parameters.

2. Structural Welding & Alignment

Frames undergo precise laser welding and protective primer coatings to withstand high atmospheric salinity.

3. Pressure & Vacuum Checks

Evaporator coils undergo strict bubble emission testing inside specialized water chambers under high pneumatic pressure.

4. Final Load Profiling

Chillers run on full load inside our testing chamber to evaluate compressor temperature, flow rates, and electronic sensor readouts.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear information regarding lead times, product customization, and maintenance parameters.

Are AAS industrial chillers customizable?

Yes. Every scroll, screw, or reciprocating chiller model is configured as per specific project load parameters, fluid flow rates, customized physical dimensions, paint colors, and integrated pump configurations.

What is the typical lead time for AHU or Duct manufacturing?

Standard orders for air ducts and standard capacity AHUs are dispatched within 2 to 3 weeks. Custom high-capacity double-skinned AHUs or multi-compressor screw chiller setups take around 4 to 6 weeks, including rigorous vacuum and load testing cycles.

What safety controls are integrated within Bulk Milk Coolers?

AAS Bulk Milk Coolers are equipped with an advanced digital anti-freezing protection system, automatic high-precision agitators, phase failure relays, overload protection switches, and dual redundancy compressors to guarantee zero product loss.

Does AAS offer pan-India maintenance and installation support?

Yes, we have field engineers stationed across major hubs in India. We offer installation supervision, startup trials, and structured annual maintenance contracts (AMCs) with 24/7 technical support.
